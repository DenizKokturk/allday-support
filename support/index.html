Help & Support

How can we help?

Find quick answers for reminders, imports, subscriptions and Allday+ purchases.

Contact support

If the answer is not below, email deniz202.kokturk@gmail.com. Please include your Allday version and iOS version, but do not send private notes, contact data or payment details.

Reminders are not appearing

Open Settings in Allday and check notification access and Reminder Health. Also confirm that notifications are enabled for Allday in iOS Settings and that Focus or Scheduled Summary is not delaying them.

Importing birthdays or calendar dates

Allday asks for Contacts or Calendar permission only after you choose an import action. If access was denied, open iOS Settings → Apps → Allday and update the permission, then return to the import screen. Imports remain selectable; Allday does not silently import everything.

Restoring Allday+

Open the Allday+ screen and choose Restore Purchases. Use the same Apple Account that originally purchased the subscription. Restoring does not charge you again.

Managing or cancelling a subscription

Purchases are billed and managed by Apple. Open iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions to change or cancel an Allday+ subscription.

Deleting your Allday data

You can delete individual dates and subscriptions inside Allday. Removing the app deletes its local app data from that device. If you need help with a data request, contact support.

Before reporting a problem

  • Update Allday and iOS to the latest available version.
  • Restart Allday and repeat the action once.
  • Describe the screen, the action you took and what happened.
  • Do not include birthdays, private notes, contact names or payment information.
