Privacy Policy

Effective July 21, 2026

Allday is built to help you remember important dates while keeping your personal information under your control. This policy explains what information Allday uses and why.

Information stored by Allday

Information you add—such as titles, dates, reminder choices, subscription renewal details, prices and app preferences—is stored in the app on your device. Allday’s CloudKit synchronization is not currently enabled.

Contacts and Calendar

Allday requests Contacts or Calendar access only after you choose the corresponding import or export action. Contact birthdays and calendar events are shown for your selection and are not silently imported. Calendar write access is used only when you choose to add an Allday date to Apple Calendar.

Notifications and widgets

If you enable reminders, Allday schedules local notifications on your device. Widget content is written to Allday’s private App Group container so the widget extension can display the dates you choose. Private notes, contact links and subscription prices are not written to the widget snapshot.

Purchases and RevenueCat

Allday+ purchases are processed by Apple. Allday uses RevenueCat to retrieve available products, verify subscription entitlement status, restore purchases and provide subscription management features. Apple and RevenueCat may process purchase identifiers, product information, subscription status and an app-specific customer identifier. Allday does not receive or store your payment card details.

Advertising and tracking

Allday does not sell your personal information and does not use third-party advertising SDKs. Allday does not use your dates, contacts or subscription details for targeted advertising.

Website information

This website is hosted by GitHub Pages. GitHub may process standard server information such as IP address, browser type and request logs when you visit. GitHub’s handling of that information is governed by the GitHub Privacy Statement.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete dates and subscription records from within Allday. You can remove local app data by deleting the app from your device. Purchase records remain subject to Apple’s and RevenueCat’s retention obligations. For assistance with a privacy request, contact us using the address below.

Children

Allday is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children through an online account or advertising service.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when Allday’s features or service providers change. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are published.
